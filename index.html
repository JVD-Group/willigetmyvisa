What you can do next to strengthen your application

  • Start by filling in your personal details exactly as per your passport.

This is not immigration advice, not a guarantee of visa approval, and not an insurance product. The score here is a simple prototype based only on completion of sections. A real engine would combine anonymised historical data, document checks and country-specific rules.
